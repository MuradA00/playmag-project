About us

Perfumedor S.L. is a registered company based in Spain that exclusively collaborates with official distributors, large wholesalers, and publishers.

Our primary focus is on wholesale and distribution of digital products, which includes games, gaming subscriptions, and gift cards. For the past three years, we
have been dedicated to meeting the unique requirements and objectives of our clients, and strive to deliver solutions that exceed their expectations. We value every partnership and consider each contractor to be important to us.

Currently, we have over 100 business partners from all around the world that we work with regularly, and we are always looking to expand our network.
